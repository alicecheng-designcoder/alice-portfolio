About

"Designing with AI as a creative collaborator, not just a tool."

I'm Alice Cheng, a designer running an AI-driven design experiment lab based in Taipei. This portfolio is a living record of design projects built entirely through AI-assisted workflows — exploring how generative and agentic AI tools reshape the way we research, ideate, prototype, and ship.

Each project tests a different hypothesis: how much of the design process can be accelerated, augmented, or reimagined when AI becomes a co-creator.

Ongoing experiments — updated continuously.
